## Mangal Dosha Kya Hota Hai?

Mangal Dosha, jo ki Kuja Dosha ya Angarak Dosha ke naam se bhi jaana jaata hai, ek astrological condition hai jo tab hoti hai jab Mangal (Mars) grah kundli ke specific houses mein baitha ho. Ye dosha shaadi aur married life par negative effect daal sakta hai.

Mangal dosha ko bahut serious liya jaata hai, especially shaadi ke time. Isliye kundli matching mein iska special attention diya jaata hai.

## Mangal Dosha Kaise Pehchana Jaata Hai?

Mangal dosha tab hota hai jab Mangal grah in houses mein ho:

- **1st House (Lagna)**: Mangal in first house
- **4th House**: Mangal in fourth house
- **7th House**: Mangal in seventh house
- **8th House**: Mangal in eighth house
- **12th House**: Mangal in twelfth house

Agar Mangal in mein se kisi bhi house mein ho, to Mangal dosha hota hai.

## Mangal Dosha Ke Types

Mangal dosha ke different types hote hain:

1. **Full Mangal Dosha**: Agar Mangal 1st, 4th, 7th, 8th, ya 12th house mein ho
2. **Partial Mangal Dosha**: Agar Mangal in houses mein ho but weak position mein
3. **Cancelled Mangal Dosha**: Agar Mangal dosha cancel ho jaye kisi yog se

## Mangal Dosha Ke Effects

Mangal dosha ke different effects ho sakte hain:

- **Marriage Problems**: Delayed marriage, compatibility issues
- **Health Issues**: Accidents, injuries, blood-related problems
- **Financial Problems**: Money loss, property disputes
- **Relationship Issues**: Conflicts, misunderstandings

## Mangal Dosha Cancellation

Mangal dosha cancel ho sakta hai agar:

- **Mangal in Own House**: Mangal apni own rashi mein ho (Mesh ya Vrishchik)
- **Mangal with Guru**: Mangal aur Guru ek saath ho
- **Mangal with Shukra**: Mangal aur Shukra ek saath ho
- **Both Have Mangal Dosha**: Dono partners mein Mangal dosha ho

## Mangal Dosha Ke Upay

Mangal dosha ke liye various upay hain:

1. **Mangal Mantra**: "Om Mangalaya Namah" - 108 times daily
2. **Gemstone**: Moonga (Red Coral) pehnein after consultation
3. **Puja**: Mangal grah ki puja karein
4. **Daan**: Red items, copper, jaggery daan karein
5. **Fasting**: Tuesday ko fast rakhein
6. **Remedies**: Specific remedies astrologer se puchhein

## Mangal Dosha aur Marriage

Mangal dosha marriage mein bahut important hai:

- **Kundli Matching**: Mangal dosha matching mein check hota hai
- **Compatibility**: Mangal dosha compatibility affect karta hai
- **Remedies**: Proper remedies se problems solve ho sakti hain
- **Both Partners**: Dono partners ka Mangal dosha check karna chahiye

## Modern Perspective

Aajkal bahut log Mangal dosha ko seriously nahi lete, lekin traditional astrology mein iska bahut importance hai. Important hai ki proper analysis karke remedies follow kiye jayein.

## Conclusion

Mangal dosha ek serious astrological condition hai, lekin proper remedies se iske effects ko minimize kiya ja sakta hai. Important hai ki experienced astrologer se consultation lein aur proper upay follow karein.
